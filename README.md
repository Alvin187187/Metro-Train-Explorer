# Metro-Train-Explorer
Everyday, a lot of Filipinos rely on our public transportations. One of those are the three mighty lines of LRT and MRT that connect each corner of Metro Manila. Well, Metro Manila is a relatively huge metropolitan area that comprises complex areas and territories that makes the navigation hard especially to our commuters and also for the tourists.

This app aims to guide our commuters throughout their journey. By providing them salient information to keep their journey safe and fun! It provides a fare list and an interactive map that pins the stations of our train lines. It could also provide an entertainment for its users by having a mini-trivia game with regards to train lines of Metro Manila.
